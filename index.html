Our Story

Nestled in the rolling hills of Carroll County, Georgia, Abilene Oaks Farm is a family-owned operation dedicated to raising healthy hens the old-fashioned way. Our chickens spend their days outdoors, foraging on grass and enjoying the sunshine — resulting in eggs with rich flavor and vibrant yolks.
