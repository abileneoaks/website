Our Story

Nestled in the rolling hills of Carrollton, Georgia, Abilene Oaks Farm is a family-owned operation dedicated to raising happy, healthy hens the old-fashioned way. Our chickens roam freely under the shade of beautiful oak trees, foraging on fresh grass and enjoying the Georgia sunshine. The result? Eggs with vibrant golden yolks, rich flavor, and the wholesome goodness that only comes from truly pasture-raised hens.
